# Ethical Backlink Acquisition Roadmap

Goal: build toward roughly 500 genuine backlink opportunities over 6-12 months. This is a prospect and relationship goal, not a guarantee of 500 acquired links.

## Month 1

- Fix the `/jobs` canonical/title/meta issue.
- Publish or improve 3-5 link-worthy resource pages.
- Review the first 50 high-priority prospects in `backlink_prospects.csv`.
- Manually contact 10-15 prospects after personalizing each email.
- Track every contact date, follow-up date, response, and acquired link.

## Months 2-3

- Expand outreach to 150 total prospects.
- Focus on universities, NGOs, employers listed on Somken Jobs, chambers, and professional associations.
- Create 4-6 more resource pages from the content plan.
- Review response quality and improve templates based on real replies.
- Add organization/employer profile pages if not already available.

## Months 4-6

- Expand to 300 legitimate prospects.
- Add data-driven resources such as monthly hiring reports and sector directories.
- Begin media/blog outreach only when there is useful data or a strong story.
- Ask listed organizations to review and reference their profile pages where helpful.
- Review acquired links monthly for relevance and quality.

## Months 7-12

- Expand toward 500 legitimate prospects.
- Build recurring partnerships with universities, NGOs, chambers, and associations.
- Refresh resource pages quarterly so they remain link-worthy.
- Continue manual outreach in small, personalized batches.
- Avoid any offer, agreement, or pattern that looks like paid placement or a link scheme.

## Operating Rules

- Outreach should always be manual or reviewed by a human before sending.
- Every prospect needs a natural reason to link.
- Prefer branded and natural anchors such as `Somken Jobs`, `East Africa jobs resource`, or `Somalia NGO jobs resource`.
- Do not demand exact-match anchors.
- Do not pressure organizations into reciprocal links.
- Do not use automated account creation, comment posting, or directory submissions.

## Monthly Review

Track:
- Prospects contacted
- Replies received
- Links acquired
- Resource pages improved
- Broken or removed links
- Search Console impressions/clicks for target pages
- Job posting rich result issues

Stop outreach to any category that produces low-quality links or poor-fit responses.
